How the warranty process works

  1. Register at delivery. Submit the registration form when you take delivery so your warranty is validated and your unit is on file.
  2. Report an issue. If a covered problem appears, file a claim with your serial number and photos before any repair work begins.
  3. Get authorization. We review the claim and issue a warranty authorization number (and a purchase order when applicable).
  4. Complete the repair. Once authorized, proceed with the repair and send your invoice referencing the trailer serial number and the warranty number.
