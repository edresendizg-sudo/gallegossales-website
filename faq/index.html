Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about buying Gallegos semi-trailers — trailer types, materials, pricing and how to get a quote.

Where can I buy Gallegos trailers?

You can buy Gallegos-built semi-trailers directly through Gallegos Trailer Sales in Laredo, Texas. Call or WhatsApp 956-378-5818 for a factory-direct quote, or use our contact form. We serve customers across Texas, the Permian Basin and the United States.

What types of trailers does Gallegos Trailer Sales offer?

We offer four main categories: scrap metal & demolition dump trailers (HARDOX 450 end dumps), vacuum tank trailers for oilfield and liquid hauling (lightweight aluminum or stainless), dry bulk pneumatic trailers, and belly dump & aggregate trailers.

Do you sell aluminum vacuum tank trailers for the oilfield?

Yes. Our vacuum tank trailers are available in lightweight aluminum or stainless steel (ASTM A36), 130–150 Bbl, and are certified to USDOT 406, ASME and R-Stamp standards — built for produced water hauling, oilfield mud and chemical waste in the Permian Basin and beyond.

What is a HARDOX 450 dump trailer?

HARDOX 450 is a high-strength, abrasion-resistant steel. Our demolition and scrap metal end dump trailers are built with HARDOX 450 and a reinforced quarter frame, with 80–96 yd³ capacity and a choice of spring ride or air ride — engineered to haul demolition debris and scrap metal.

Where is Gallegos Trailer Sales located?

We are based in Laredo, Texas, and ship trailers to customers throughout Texas, the Permian Basin and the wider United States.
